## üÜï **POTENTIAL NEW SPECIES TO ADD**

### **High-Priority Species (High Search Volume + Common Mistakes):**

1. **Bearded Dragon** üî• VERY HIGH PRIORITY
   - **Search Volume:** "bearded dragon setup" ~18,100/month
   - **Why:** Very popular, many common mistakes (sand, small tanks, wrong UVB)
   - **Common Mistakes:** Calcium sand, 20-gallon tanks, coil UVB bulbs
   - **Minimum Tank:** 120 gallons (4x2x2)
   - **Heating:** Halogen/DHP + UVB required
   - **Substrate:** Tile, paper towels, or 70/30 topsoil/sand mix

2. **Ball Python** üî• HIGH PRIORITY
   - **Search Volume:** "ball python setup" ~14,800/month
   - **Why:** Very popular, specific humidity needs
   - **Common Mistakes:** Small tanks, wrong substrate, inadequate hides
   - **Minimum Tank:** 120 gallons (4x2x2) for adults
   - **Heating:** Halogen/DHP + thermostat
   - **Substrate:** Cypress mulch, coco fiber, or paper towels

3. **Crested Gecko** üî• HIGH PRIORITY
   - **Search Volume:** "crested gecko setup" ~8,100/month
   - **Why:** Popular beginner reptile, vertical setup needed
   - **Common Mistakes:** Horizontal tanks, wrong humidity
   - **Minimum Tank:** 20-gallon tall (vertical)
   - **Heating:** Usually not needed (room temp OK)
   - **Substrate:** Paper towels, coco fiber, or bioactive

4. **Goldfish** ‚ö†Ô∏è MEDIUM PRIORITY
   - **Search Volume:** "goldfish tank setup" ~12,100/month
   - **Why:** Very common, many misconceptions (bowls, small tanks)
   - **Common Mistakes:** Bowls, 10-gallon tanks, no filter
   - **Minimum Tank:** 20 gallons for one, 10 gallons per additional
   - **Filtration:** Heavy filtration required
   - **Heating:** Usually not needed (cold water)

5. **Axolotl** ‚ö†Ô∏è MEDIUM PRIORITY
   - **Search Volume:** "axolotl setup" ~6,600/month
   - **Why:** Growing popularity, specific needs
   - **Common Mistakes:** Wrong temperature, sharp decor, small tanks
   - **Minimum Tank:** 20 gallons
   - **Temperature:** 60-68¬∞F (chiller needed in warm climates)
   - **Filtration:** Low-flow required
